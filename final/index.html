Our Story

CoffeeSpot was born from a simple dream: to create a cozy sanctuary in the midst of a busy routine. It all started in a small garage, where the aroma of fresh coffee and a passion for welcoming people inspired us to open our doors.

Today, we keep the exact same essence from day one: carefully selected beans, dedication to our craft, and an atmosphere that makes you feel right at home.
