CoffeeSpot - Site Plan

Site Name

CoffeeSpot

The name "CoffeeSpot" was selected because it is simple, memorable, and represents a cozy place to enjoy coffee.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to present a coffee shop and provide essential information such as menu, location, hours, and customer reviews.

Scenarios

  • What drinks and food are available on the menu?
  • Where is the coffee shop located and what are the opening hours?
  • What do customers think about the coffee shop?

Color Scheme

Primary Color (#6F4E37): Used for headers and titles.

Background Color (#F5F5DC): Used for the page background.

Accent Color (#D2B48C): Used for sections.

Typography

Poppins: Headings

Arial: Body text
